# Terms of Reference: Back-to-School Drive 2023

**Reference:** ADA/2023/004
**Date:** August 1 – December 31, 2023
**Location:** Bamenda & surrounding villages, North-West Region, Cameroon
**Organization:** Apoti Development Association (ADA)

---

## 1. Background

The Anglophone crisis in Cameroon's North-West Region has displaced many families, leaving children—particularly orphans—without access to education. ADA's Back-to-School Drive addresses this gap by providing full educational support to vulnerable children identified through community networks.

This is ADA's first back-to-school drive in Bamenda, expanding from previous operations in Buea to reach more affected communities.

## 2. Objective

To enroll and sustain 20 orphaned children in school for the 2023–2024 academic year by providing tuition, uniforms, school supplies, and ongoing monitoring through partnerships with local schools and community leaders.

## 3. Scope of Work

### 3.1 Beneficiary Identification
- Partner with local community leaders to identify the most vulnerable orphaned children
- Verify eligibility and prioritize based on need

### 3.2 Educational Support
- Pay tuition fees for 20 children across 4 partner schools
- Provide complete school uniforms
- Supply books and school materials

### 3.3 Monitoring & Follow-up
- Conduct regular village visits to monitor attendance and performance
- Coordinate with school administrators on student progress
- Provide ongoing support throughout the academic year

## 4. Target Beneficiaries

- **Number:** 20 orphaned children
- **Location:** Bamenda and surrounding villages
- **Selection criteria:** Orphaned, displaced, or otherwise unable to access education due to the crisis

## 6. Partner Schools

4 local schools in Bamenda and surrounding villages (identified through community leader networks).
