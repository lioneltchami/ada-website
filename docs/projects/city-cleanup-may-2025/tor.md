## 1. Background

The Apoti Development Association (ADA) conducts an annual Labour Day community cleanup as part of its environmental stewardship and community service mandate. The 2025 edition marks the third consecutive year of this initiative, building on successful campaigns in 2023 (12 volunteers) and 2024.

## 2. Objective

To mobilize ADA volunteers for a one-day intensive cleanup of the commercial district of Buea, demonstrating environmental responsibility and raising community awareness about waste management.

## 3. Scope of Work

- Sweep streets and sidewalks in the commercial area of Buea
- Shovel debris and collect waste
- Deploy ADA-branded trash bins for ongoing community use
- Promote environmental stewardship through visible, branded community action

## 4. Team Composition

- **Volunteers:** 15 (largest team to date)
- **Uniform:** ADA white-and-green polo shirts, green vests, face masks, red rubber gloves

## 5. Equipment & Materials

- 5 large ADA-branded black trash bins (inscribed: "APOTI DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION — COMMUNITY SERVICE: CARING FOR OUR ENVIRONMENT, SERVING WITH LOVE.")
- Cleaning tools: shovels, brooms, rakes
- Protective gear: face masks, rubber gloves, vests
- Waste disposal truck

## 6. Target Beneficiaries

~300 community members (indirect) — residents, traders, and visitors in the commercial district of Buea.

## 7. Expected Outcomes

1. Streets cleaned in the commercial district
2. ADA-branded bins deployed for ongoing use
3. Full volunteer team in professional uniform
4. Waste collected and properly disposed
5. Annual tradition continued and strengthened
6. Environmental stewardship demonstrated publicly
7. Community awareness raised on waste management
