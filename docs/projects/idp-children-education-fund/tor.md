# Terms of Reference: IDP Children Education Fund

**Reference:** ADA/2025/006
**Date:** September 1, 2025 – Ongoing
**Status:** ONGOING
**Location:** Bamenda, North-West Region, Cameroon

---

## 1. Background

The Anglophone crisis in Cameroon's North-West and South-West regions has displaced thousands of families from their homes. Many parents fled conflict zones with nothing, leaving them unable to afford basic education for their children. Without intervention, these children face permanent educational exclusion, compounding the long-term impact of displacement on their futures.

## 2. Project Objective

To provide full educational sponsorship for 15 internally displaced children in Bamenda, ensuring their enrollment and retention in local schools for the 2025–2026 academic year, complemented by psychosocial support to address trauma from displacement.

## 3. Scope of Work

### 3.1 Education Support
- Payment of school fees for 15 IDP children
- Provision of school uniforms
- Supply of textbooks and learning materials

### 3.2 Psychosocial Support
- Facilitated group sessions addressing displacement-related trauma
- Emotional wellbeing monitoring throughout the school year

### 3.3 Coordination
- Liaison with schools to ensure enrollment and attendance tracking
- Regular communication with families on children's progress

## 4. Beneficiaries

- **Direct:** 15 children from internally displaced families
- **Indirect:** 15 families (parents/guardians) relieved of education cost burden
