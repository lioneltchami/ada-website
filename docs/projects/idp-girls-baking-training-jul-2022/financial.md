## 5. Variance Analysis

### Over-budget items:

| Item | Variance | Explanation |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Training ingredients | +10,000 CFA | Egg and butter prices increased 15% in mid-July due to supply disruption. Additional purchases required for Week 3 production runs. |

### Under-budget items:

| Item | Variance | Explanation |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Transport | -18,000 CFA | 11 of 15 participants lived within walking distance of venue. Only 4 girls required transport support, and 2 found closer accommodation by Week 3. |
| Communication | -7,000 CFA | WhatsApp groups eliminated need for individual calls. Volunteers used personal phones. |
| Startup kits | -5,000 CFA | Bulk purchase of cooking oil (15 units) secured 500 CFA/unit discount from wholesale distributor. |
| Contingency | -5,000 CFA | No emergencies or unexpected costs arose. |

## 7. Financial Controls

- All expenditures approved by ADA project coordinator before disbursement
- Receipts collected for all purchases above 5,000 CFA
- Market purchases witnessed by at least 2 ADA volunteers
- Weekly reconciliation of cash against receipts
- Startup kit procurement verified by inventory count (15 complete kits confirmed)
- Instructor payments made weekly upon completion of training sessions
