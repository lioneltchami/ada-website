## 1. Executive Summary

The Apoti Development Association (ADA) successfully delivered its largest event to date—a two-day Young Women Empowerment Conference held on October 27–28, 2024, at a church venue in Buea, South-West Region, Cameroon. Over 80 young women and girls attended the faith-based conference, which featured teaching sessions on faith, purpose, and personal development led by two female speakers. The event was delivered within budget at 1,180,000 CFA ($1,967), under the approved budget of 1,200,000 CFA ($2,000).

## 2. Background and Context

ADA has been empowering vulnerable communities in Cameroon since 2021 through faith-based programs supporting widows, orphans, and young women. This conference was conceived as a flagship event to reach young women and girls with messages of empowerment, faith, and purpose. It represents a significant milestone as ADA's largest single event.

## 6. Key Outcomes

1. **Largest ADA event to date** — 80+ beneficiaries in a single event
2. **Two-day conference delivered** — Full program executed as planned
3. **Two female speakers led sessions** — Including a woman in traditional beaded Cameroonian necklace and another in colorful blouse, representing local culture

## 7. Challenges and Lessons Learned

| Challenge | Mitigation/Lesson |
