## 1. Background

The Apoti Development Association (ADA) has conducted annual Christmas giving campaigns since 2022, bringing joy and essential supplies to vulnerable children in Cameroon. The 2024 edition marks the third annual campaign and the largest to date, expanding from 30 children (2022) to 50 children. This year's campaign moves beyond a simple drop-off to a full celebration program at a children's home in Buea.

## 2. Objectives

1. Provide Christmas gifts, food supplies, and treats to 50 orphaned children
2. Deliver a full celebration program including singing, worship, and interactive activities
3. Create emotional connection and demonstrate love to vulnerable children
4. Strengthen ADA's branded community presence through volunteer engagement

## 3. Scope of Work

### 3.1 Procurement
- Food supplies: cooking oil (Mayor brand), rice, soap, toilet paper, juice/drinks
- Toys and gifts for 50 children
- Treats, snacks, and pastries
- Santa hats and decorations for volunteers and venue

### 3.2 Program Delivery
- Organize transport and delivery of all supplies to the orphanage
- Conduct a full Christmas celebration program:
  - Singing and clapping
  - Worship session
  - Interactive activities with children
- Distribute gifts, food supplies, and treats to all 50 children

### 3.3 Branding & Documentation
- 8 ADA volunteers in branded white polo shirts ("Love, Respect & Service") and Santa hats
- Photography and documentation of the event
- Communication and coordination with orphanage staff

## 4. Team Composition

- **Volunteers:** 8 ADA members
- **Attire:** Branded white polo shirts with "Love, Respect & Service" motto + Santa hats

## 5. Expected Outcomes

- 50 children receive Christmas gifts, food supplies, and treats
- Full celebration program delivered (singing, worship, interactive activities)
- Food supplies delivered to orphanage (cooking oil, rice, soap, toilet paper, juice)
- Emotional connection established with orphaned children
- ADA branded presence maintained throughout event
- Event documented for reporting and future campaigns
