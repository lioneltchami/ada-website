## 1. Background

The Apoti Development Association (ADA) has historically focused its mentorship and empowerment programs on girls, young women, and widows. Recognizing that orphaned and vulnerable boys in the community also lack access to positive male role models, life skills training, and spiritual guidance, ADA initiated its first dedicated youth mentorship program for boys and male adolescents.

This program was developed in partnership with Full Gospel Mission Church, Buea, whose 2025 mission theme — "Working Together in the Power of the Holy Spirit in Our Communities" — aligns with ADA's values of love, respect, and service.

## 2. Objectives

1. Provide faith-based mentorship to 30 young boys and male adolescents (ages 8–20) from vulnerable backgrounds
2. Deliver life skills training to equip participants for personal development and responsible citizenship
3. Facilitate Bible study sessions to strengthen spiritual foundations
4. Establish ADA's youth mentorship program for boys as a recurring initiative
5. Strengthen partnership with Full Gospel Mission Church for community outreach

## 3. Target Beneficiaries

- **Number:** 30 young boys and male adolescents
- **Age range:** 8–20 years
- **Profile:** Orphaned, vulnerable, and at-risk male youth in the Buea community

## 4. Activities

| Day | Activities |
|-----|-----------|
| Day 1 (Oct 26) | Opening session, facilitator presentations (laptop-based), Bible study, life skills training, refreshments |

### Key Components

- Laptop-based presentations by 3 facilitators on life skills and faith topics
- Interactive Bible study sessions (participants provided with Bibles)
- Note-taking exercises (notebooks and pens provided)
- Mentorship discussions and Q&A
- Documentation and group photography

## 7. Expected Outcomes

- 30 male youth receive mentorship and life skills training over 2 days
- Participants gain spiritual grounding through Bible study
- ADA establishes a model for boys' mentorship programming
- Church partnership strengthened for future community initiatives
- Program documented for reporting and replication

## 9. Sustainability

This seminar serves as a pilot for ADA's boys' mentorship track. Successful delivery will inform:
- Quarterly mentorship sessions for male youth
- Peer mentorship pairing
- Follow-up programs with Full Gospel Mission Church
