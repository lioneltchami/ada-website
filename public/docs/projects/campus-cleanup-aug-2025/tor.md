## 1. Background

The Apoti Development Association (ADA) has been conducting environmental cleanup initiatives in Buea as part of its community service mandate. Following successful street cleanup campaigns (including the May 2025 city cleanup), ADA identified the need to extend this model to educational institutions, reaching the student population with messages of civic duty and environmental responsibility.

## 2. Objective

To conduct an environmental cleanup at a student campus in Buea, promoting environmental responsibility among students and extending ADA's cleanup model from city streets to educational institutions.

### Specific Objectives

- Clean campus grounds and collect waste
- Deploy ADA-branded trash bins for sustained impact
- Raise environmental awareness among the student population
- Demonstrate ADA's commitment to community service in educational settings

## 3. Target Beneficiaries

- **Direct:** 8 ADA volunteers
- **Indirect:** ~500 students on campus

## 4. Scope of Work

### Activities

1. Environmental cleanup of campus grounds
2. Waste collection and proper disposal
3. Deployment of ADA-branded black trash bins labeled: *"APOTI DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION — COMMUNITY SERVICE: CARING FOR OUR ENVIRONMENT"*
4. Promotion of environmental responsibility among students
5. Documentation of activities (photos/video)

### Team Composition

- 8 ADA volunteers in ADA uniforms and red rubber gloves

## 5. Expected Outcomes

- Campus grounds cleaned and waste properly disposed
- ADA-branded bins placed on campus for ongoing use
- Student awareness of environmental responsibility raised
- ADA cleanup model successfully extended to educational institutions
- Volunteers visible in protective gear and branded uniforms
