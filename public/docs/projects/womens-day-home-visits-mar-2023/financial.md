## 5. Variance Analysis

Total project expenditure was 15,000 CFA ($25.00 USD) under budget, representing a 4.2% savings. The following factors contributed to the positive variance:

### 5.1 Rice Procurement (−4,000 CFA)

Budgeted at 12,000 CFA per 10kg bag based on retail pricing. Purchasing 12 bags from a single vendor at the market yielded a wholesale discount, reducing the effective per-bag cost to approximately 11,667 CFA.

### 5.2 Cooking Oil Procurement (−3,000 CFA)

Similarly, bulk purchase of 12 × 5L jerry cans from one supplier yielded a modest wholesale discount, reducing total cost from 60,000 to 57,000 CFA.

### 5.3 Buckets & Seasonings (−2,000 CFA)

Orange buckets were purchased at the budgeted rate, but assorted seasonings to fill them were sourced slightly below estimated cost through market negotiation.

### 5.4 Communication Savings (−2,000 CFA)

WhatsApp messaging (using existing data bundles) reduced the need for voice call airtime. Coordination was efficiently managed through the team's WhatsApp group.

### 5.5 Contingency Underspend (−4,000 CFA)

The contingency line of 9,000 CFA was conservatively budgeted. Actual miscellaneous expenses (water for team, minor incidentals) totaled only 5,000 CFA.

### 5.6 Conclusion

The under-spend reflects effective procurement practices (wholesale purchasing) and efficient communication methods, not a reduction in planned activities. All 12 widows received complete food packages as planned.
