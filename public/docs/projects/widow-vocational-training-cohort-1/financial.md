## 5. Variance Analysis

### Savings (Total: -157,000 CFA)

- **Stipends (-72,000):** Three beneficiaries dropped out at months 3, 4, and 5 respectively, eliminating their remaining stipend payments.
- **Starter kits (-45,000):** Only 12 kits procured and distributed instead of planned 15.
- **Contingency (-25,000):** No major unforeseen expenses materialized.
- **Materials (-15,000):** Bulk purchasing in month 1 secured better unit prices than budgeted.

### Overruns (Total: +7,000 CFA)

- **Transport (+5,000):** Additional field visits required for dropout follow-up and beneficiary support.
- **Graduation (+2,000):** Slightly higher catering costs due to additional community guests.

### Net Variance: -150,000 CFA vs. line-item budget; -50,000 CFA vs. approved budget of 1,800,000.
