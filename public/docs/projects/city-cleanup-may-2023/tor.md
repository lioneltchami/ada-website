## 1. Background

Every year on Labour Day (May 1), ADA organizes a community environmental cleanup in Buea to demonstrate its commitment to community health beyond individual aid. Urban Buea faces persistent sanitation challenges including illegal dump sites, clogged drainage channels, and waste accumulation between residential buildings. This initiative tackles these issues through collective volunteer action.

## 2. Objectives

- Clear illegal dump sites along main roads in Buea
- Unblock drainage channels clogged with waste
- Remove garbage from between residential buildings
- Raise environmental awareness among community members
- Demonstrate ADA's community service model

## 3. Target Beneficiaries

Approximately 200 community members as indirect beneficiaries of a cleaner, healthier environment.

## 4. Activities

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Dump site clearance | Clear illegal dump sites along main roads |
| Drainage unblocking | Unblock drainage channels clogged with waste |
| Residential cleanup | Remove garbage from between residential buildings |
| Waste disposal | Hire truck to transport collected waste to proper disposal site |

## 5. Team Composition

12 volunteers equipped with:
- ADA branded polo shirts
- Green caps
- Face masks
- Red rubber gloves
- Shovels and hand tools

## 6. Expected Outcomes

- Streets and drainage channels cleaned and functional
- Illegal dump sites cleared
- Waste removed from residential areas
- Community health conditions improved
- Environmental awareness raised among residents
- ADA community service model demonstrated publicly
