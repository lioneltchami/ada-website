# Terms of Reference: Back-to-School Bamenda 2024

| Field | Details |

| **Project Title** | Back-to-School Drive — Bamenda 2024 |
| **Location** | Bamenda, North-West Region, Cameroon |
| **Duration** | September 1–30, 2024 |
| **Budget** | $1,200 USD (~720,000 CFA) |
| **Beneficiaries** | 15 young children (ages 3–12) |
| **Implementing Organization** | Apoti Development Association (ADA) |

## 1. Background

The Anglophone crisis in Cameroon's North-West Region has displaced thousands of families, leaving many unable to afford basic education for their children. In Bamenda, very young children — some as young as 3 years old — have never attended school due to the ongoing instability and economic hardship faced by their families.

This project represents ADA's second year of back-to-school intervention in Bamenda, expanding the organization's reach to crisis-affected communities in the North-West Region.

## 2. Objectives

1. Provide 15 crisis-affected children (ages 3–12) with complete school supply kits to enable school enrollment.
2. Remove financial barriers to education by contributing toward school fees.
3. Strengthen ADA's presence in Bamenda and build community relationships.
4. Offer spiritual encouragement to beneficiary families through prayer and worship.

## 3. Target Beneficiaries

- 15 young children aged 3–12 years
- Children from families displaced or economically affected by the Anglophone crisis
- Children who have never attended school or dropped out due to crisis
- Located in Bamenda, North-West Region

## 4. Activities

| # | Activity | Timeline |
|---|----------|----------|
| 1 | Beneficiary identification and registration | Week 1 |
| 2 | Procurement of school supplies (backpacks, textbooks, exercise books, stationery) | Week 1–2 |
| 3 | Venue preparation (community building) | Week 3 |
| 4 | Distribution event with prayer/worship session | Week 3–4 |
| 5 | School fees payment to respective schools | Week 4 |

## 5. Deliverables

- 15 branded navy blue school backpacks (with school logos)
- 15 sets of textbooks
- 15 sets of exercise books (10 per child)
- 15 stationery kits (pens, pencils, ruler, eraser)
- School fees contribution for all 15 children

- Strengthened community trust in ADA's mission in Bamenda
