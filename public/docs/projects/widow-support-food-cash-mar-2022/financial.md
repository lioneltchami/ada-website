## 5. Notes

1. **Under-budget delivery:** The project was completed 20,000 CFA under budget due to favorable pricing on rice and cooking oil purchased in bulk, and lower-than-expected transport costs.
2. **Cash envelopes:** All 15 cash envelopes of 3,000 CFA each were distributed as planned. Beneficiaries signed receipt acknowledgments.
3. **Contingency usage:** Only 15,000 CFA of the 22,500 CFA contingency was utilized, primarily for additional transport needs during street visits.
4. **Remaining funds:** 20,000 CFA returned to ADA General Fund.
5. **Exchange rate:** USD equivalents are approximate based on prevailing rate of ~600 CFA per USD in March 2022.
