## 1. Executive Summary

On May 1, 2025, the Apoti Development Association (ADA) successfully conducted its third annual Labour Day community cleanup in the commercial district of Buea. A team of 15 volunteers — the largest to date — cleaned streets, collected waste, and deployed branded trash bins across the area. The project was completed within budget, spending 290,000 CFA of the 300,000 CFA allocated.

## 2. Context

This was ADA's third consecutive Labour Day cleanup, demonstrating growing organizational capacity:

| Year | Volunteers | Notes |
|------|:----------:|-------|
| 2023 | 12 | First edition |
| 2024 | — | Continued tradition |
| 2025 | 15 | Largest team, professional branded equipment |

## 3. Activities Conducted

1. **Street sweeping** — Volunteers swept streets and sidewalks throughout the commercial district
2. **Debris removal** — Shoveled accumulated debris from gutters and walkways
3. **Waste collection** — Gathered waste into ADA-branded bins and bags for disposal
4. **Bin deployment** — Placed 5 large branded black trash bins in strategic locations
5. **Waste disposal** — Hired truck transported all collected waste to proper disposal site
