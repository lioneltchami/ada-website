## 1. Executive Summary

The Widow Support Program Cohort 3 is progressing on schedule through its fourth month. All 20 beneficiaries remain enrolled across both Bamenda and Douala sites. Monthly food distributions and stipend payments have been delivered consistently. Vocational training is underway with widows developing skills in tailoring and hairdressing. The expansion to Douala has been successfully established.
