# Safeguarding Policy

**Apoti Development Association (ADA)**  
Registration: N° 415/G.37/D14/VolI/SAAJP  
Effective date: January 2024 · Review: Annual

## Purpose

ADA is committed to protecting children, young people, and vulnerable adults who participate in our programs. This policy applies to all staff, volunteers, partners, and visitors.

## Scope

- All program activities involving minors or vulnerable adults
- Partner organizations delivering activities on ADA’s behalf
- Digital communications and photography involving beneficiaries

## Core commitments

1. **Zero tolerance** for abuse, exploitation, harassment, or neglect.
2. **Safe recruitment** — reference checks and safeguarding briefing for roles with beneficiary contact.
3. **Reporting** — concerns reported within 24 hours to the Safeguarding Focal Point (`info@apotidev.org`).
4. **Confidentiality** — information shared only with those who need to know for protection.
5. **Photography & consent** — no identifying images of minors without guardian consent.

## Reporting

Anyone who witnesses or suspects harm should contact:

- **Email:** info@apotidev.org (subject: Safeguarding)
- **Phone:** +237 676 282 346

Serious cases are referred to relevant Cameroon authorities and, where applicable, reported to institutional funders.

## Review

The Board reviews this policy annually. Updates are published in the resource library at [apotidev.org/resources](/resources).
